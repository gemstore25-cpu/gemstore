FAQs
How long does diamond delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 5-15 minutes after payment confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, Razorpay, Cashfree, and Wallet balance.

How do I find my MLBB User ID and Server ID?

Open Mobile Legends, go to your Profile. User ID is shown at the top. Server ID is the number in parentheses next to your name.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available for failed orders. Contact support via WhatsApp or ticket system.
